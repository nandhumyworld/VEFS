Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

Not at all! While some opportunities may benefit from specific skills, most positions welcome enthusiastic beginners. We provide training and mentorship to help you succeed in your role.

Are food and accommodation provided?

Benefits vary by opportunity. Many full-time, on-site positions include meals and accommodation. Check each opportunity's details under "Benefits" to see what's included. Remote and weekend positions typically don't include these benefits.

Will I receive a stipend?

Some positions offer modest stipends to cover basic expenses (typically ‚Çπ2,000-5,000/month depending on the role). All volunteer positions provide valuable learning experiences and certificates. Stipend details are clearly mentioned in each opportunity description.

How do I apply for a volunteer position?

Click on any opportunity card above to view full details, then click "Apply Now" to fill out the application form. You'll need to provide your contact information, age, motivation, and relevant experience. We'll review your application and contact you within 5-7 business days.

What if I can only volunteer on weekends?

We have flexible options! Look for opportunities marked as "Weekend" or "Part-time" commitment. These are designed specifically for working professionals and students who want to contribute without full-time availability.

Can international volunteers apply?

Yes! We welcome international volunteers. Please note that you'll need to arrange your own visa (tourist visa is usually sufficient for short-term volunteering) and travel. Remote positions are open to anyone with internet access. Contact us at vefsfoundation@gmail.com for more details.
