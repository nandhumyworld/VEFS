Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a training?

Click on any training card to view details, then click the "Register Now" button. Fill out the registration form with your information, and you'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Is there a registration fee?

Most of our trainings are FREE, made possible through our sponsors and supporters. Some specialized or multi-day programs may have a nominal fee to cover materials and logistics. The cost is clearly mentioned in each training's details.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! Participants who complete the full training program receive a Certificate of Completion from VEFS Foundation. For certification programs, additional assessments may be required.

Are trainings available online?

We offer both in-person and online training options. Some programs are hybrid, allowing you to choose your preferred mode of participation. Check each training's details for mode availability.

Can I cancel or reschedule my registration?

Yes, you can cancel up to 48 hours before the training start date. Contact us at programs@vefs.org to reschedule or transfer your registration to another session.

What is the typical class size?

To ensure quality interaction and hands-on learning, we keep group sizes between 20-30 participants for most trainings. Field visits may have smaller groups (15-20) for safety and effectiveness.

Can't Find What You're Looking For?

We're here to help! Whether you need a custom training for your organization or want to discuss specific learning needs, reach out to our team.
