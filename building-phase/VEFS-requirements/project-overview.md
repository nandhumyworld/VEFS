# VEFS - Requirements Overview

**Full Name:** Valluvam Ecological Farming and Social Welfare Foundation
**Description:** A Trust and foundation focused on creating awareness about indigenous tree species mentioned in ancient Tamil literature, restoring ecological balance through plantation and preservation, and conducting workshops, awareness sessions, and eco-education programs for various communities.

**Created:** 2025-12-23
**Status:** In Progress
