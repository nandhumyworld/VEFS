# VEFS Donation Page Requirements

**Status:** In Progress
**Priority:** Medium
**Created:** 2025-12-23

## Page Overview

The Donation Page is designed to inspire and facilitate contributions to VEFS's mission. It emphasizes the tangible impact of donations, offers flexible giving options, and integrates payment processing for seamless transactions. The page balances emotional appeal with practical donation mechanics.

## Page Structure & Sections

### 1. Page Header
- **Status:** Required
- **Content:**
  - Page title: "Support Our Mission" or "Make a Difference"
  - Hero image or background (inspiring nature/community photo)

- **Design Notes:**
  - Emotional, inspiring tone
  - High-quality imagery showing impact
  - Text overlay for readability

### 4. Donation Form
- **Status:** Required
- **Format:** Central, prominent section
- **Layout:** Organized, step-by-step approach

#### Form Sections:

**Step 1: Select Donation Amount**
- Preset amounts (based on impact showcase):
  - ₹500, ₹2,000, ₹5,000, ₹10,000 (as buttons/radio)
  - Custom amount (text input field)
- Selected amount highlighted in golden/amber
- Clear total amount display

**Step 2: Select Donation Type**
- **One-Time Donation** (radio button, default)
- **Monthly Recurring Donation** (radio button)
  - Option to set up auto-payment
  - Cancel anytime messaging
- Optional: "Recurring donations help us plan long-term initiatives"

**Step 3: Select Donation Category (Optional but Recommended)**
- **General Fund** (supports overall mission)
- **Tree Plantation Program** (specific focus)
- **Education & Awareness** (specific focus)
- **Community Empowerment** (specific focus)
- **Women's Programs** (specific focus)
- **Emergency/Disaster Relief** (if applicable)
- Checkbox to "Let VEFS decide where it's needed most"

**Step 4: Donor Information**
- **First Name** (text input, required)
- **Last Name** (text input, required)
- **Email** (email input, required)
- **Phone** (optional)
- **Organization** (optional, for corporate donors)

**Step 5: Billing & Payment**
- **Payment Method Selection:**
  - UPI (with QR code option)
  - Credit/Debit Card
  - Net Banking
  - Google Pay / Apple Pay
  - Other digital wallets
- **Bill Address** (optional, for receipt generation)
- **Billing Email** (auto-filled from donor email)

**Step 6: Additional Options**
- **Donation Frequency Confirmation** (if recurring)
- **Gift Aid/Tax Benefit** (if applicable in region)
  - "This organization is registered for tax benefits under [applicable law]"
  - Checkbox: "I want to claim tax benefits"
- **Anonymous Donation** (checkbox)
  - "Yes, keep my donation private"
- **Newsletter Opt-in** (checkbox)
  - "Yes, send me updates about VEFS programs"
- **Terms Agreement** (checkbox, required)
  - "I agree to the terms and conditions"

#### Form Controls:
- **Submit Button:** "Donate Now" or "Complete Donation" (golden/amber, large)
- **Back Button:** Navigate back to previous step
- **Progress Indicator:** Shows step progress (Step 2 of 6, etc.)
- **Security Badge:** "Secure Payment" or similar trust signal
- **Cancel/Close:** Option to exit form

#### Form Design Notes:
- Clean, uncluttered layout
- Form validation messages
- Auto-save progress (if possible)
- Mobile-optimized: Single column, touch-friendly
- HTTPS/Security visual indicators
- Optional: Split payment option display

## Payment Integration

### Payment Gateway Integration:
- **Primary:** Razorpay, Instamojo, or similar Indian payment processor
- **Support:** Credit card, debit card, UPI, net banking, digital wallets
- **Features:**
  - Secure HTTPS encryption
  - PCI DSS compliance
  - Recurring payment support
  - Webhook integration for notifications
  - Receipt/invoice generation
  - Refund management

### Payment Flow:
1. User selects donation amount
2. Fills in donor information
3. Selects payment method
4. Redirected to payment gateway
5. Completes payment
6. Confirmation email sent
7. Returns to thank you page

### Security:
- SSL/TLS encryption
- PCI compliance badge
- Security seals on payment section
- Clear data privacy statement

## Design & Visual Approach

### Color Usage
- **Headers:** Sage green (#6B8E23)
- **Donation amount cards:** White with sage green border or accent
- **Selected amount:** Golden/amber (#FBBF24) highlight
- **CTA buttons:** Golden/amber (#FBBF24)
- **Impact cards:** Color-coded or sage green accents
- **Trust indicators:** Green for security

### Layout
- **Max content width:** 1200px
- **Form width:** 600px (centered) or full-width responsive
- **Cards grid:** 2-3 columns (responsive)
- **Section padding:** 64px vertical (desktop), 32px (mobile)

### Typography
- **Headers:** Serif, sage green
- **Impact amounts:** Large, bold numbers (48px+)
- **Form labels:** Sans-serif, 14px, bold
- **Buttons:** Sans-serif, 16px, semibold

## Responsive Design

### Desktop (1024px+)
- Full donation form displayed
- Impact cards in 2-3 column grid
- QR codes and links side-by-side
- Two-column layout for sections

### Tablet (768px - 1023px)
- Form optimized for tablet
- Impact cards in 2-column grid
- Adjusted spacing
- Touch-friendly buttons

### Mobile (< 768px)
- Single-column form layout
- Full-width input fields
- Impact cards in 1-column stack
- Large touch targets (44x44px minimum)
- QR codes sized for mobile viewing
