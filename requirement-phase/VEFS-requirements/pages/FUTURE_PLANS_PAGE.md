# VEFS Future Plans Page Requirements

**Status:** In Progress
**Priority:** Lower
**Created:** 2025-12-23

## Page Overview

The Future Plans Page articulates VEFS's vision for growth and impact over the coming years. It combines narrative storytelling about the foundation's expansion goals with a visual timeline showing planned initiatives, milestones, and long-term vision. The page inspires stakeholders and demonstrates strategic direction.

## Page Structure & Sections

### 1. Page Header
- **Status:** Required
- **Content:**
  - Page title: "Our Future Vision" or "Where We're Headed"
  - Hero image or background (inspiring nature/vision image)
  - Subheading: Forward-looking tagline (e.g., "Building a Greener, More Empowered Future")
- **Design Notes:**
  - Aspirational, hopeful tone
  - Forward-looking imagery
  - Text overlay for readability

---

### 2. Vision Statement
- **Status:** Required
- **Format:** Prominent text section
- **Content:**
  - Compelling vision statement (2-4 sentences)
  - What VEFS hopes to achieve in 5-10 years
  - Broader impact on communities and environment
  - Call-to-action to be part of the vision
- **Design Notes:**
  - Large, readable typography
  - Sage green color emphasis
  - Optional: Quoted styling

- **Format:** Flowing narrative text with visual breaks
- **Content (organized by expansion areas):**

- Existing programs and initiatives

#### Expansion Areas (Next 1-3 Years)

**Area 1: Geographic Expansion**
- Narrative: Plans to expand to new regions/states
- Specific geographic areas targeted
- Rationale: Why these areas need VEFS work
- Expected impact: Communities to be reached
- Visual: Map showing current + planned coverage

**Area 2: Program Expansion**
- New programs or initiatives to launch
- How existing programs will deepen/scale
- Target audiences to be served
- New partnerships to develop
- Expected outcomes

**Area 3: Capacity Building**
- Team expansion (staff, trainers, volunteers)
- Infrastructure development (offices, centers)
- Technology integration (CMS, analytics, etc.)
- Resource development
- Expected improvements

**Area 4: Research & Documentation**
- Indigenous knowledge documentation
- Ecological impact studies
- Program effectiveness research
- Publications and resources
- Knowledge sharing initiatives

**Area 5: Advocacy & Policy**
- Advocacy for indigenous trees in policy
- Environmental education integration in schools
- Community-driven conservation initiatives
- Partnership with government bodies
- Expected policy changes

#### Long-Term Vision (5-10+ Years)

- Grand vision for VEFS's role
- Environmental transformation goals
- Community empowerment outcomes
- National/regional influence
- Legacy and lasting impact

**Design Notes:**
- Narrative sections broken by visuals
- Icons or illustrations for each area
- Progress from current → future
- Inspirational, authentic tone

---

### 4. Interactive Timeline
- **Status:** Required
- **Format:** Visual timeline showing phases and milestones
- **Layout Options:**
  - Vertical timeline (mobile-friendly)
  - Horizontal timeline (desktop with scroll)
  - Staggered timeline (alternating left/right)

#### Timeline Structure:

**Phase 1: Immediate (Next 6-12 months)**
- Specific initiatives launching soon
- Milestones with target dates
- Team/resource allocation
- Expected outcomes

**Phase 2: Short-Term (1-3 years)**
- Major program expansions
- Geographic growth targets
- Infrastructure development
- Community impact goals
- Key partnerships to establish

**Phase 3: Medium-Term (3-5 years)**
- Significant organizational growth
- Regional leadership positioning
- Major research/documentation completion
- Policy influence initiatives
- Scaled impact metrics

**Phase 4: Long-Term (5-10+ years)**
- Vision fulfillment milestones
- National/regional influence achieved
- Institutional strength and sustainability
- Legacy and lasting systems
- Future generations' benefit

#### Milestone Details:
For each milestone:
- **Date/Period:** When it will happen
- **Initiative/Goal:** What specifically
- **Area:** Which expansion area
- **Impact:** Expected outcomes or reach
- **Status Indicator:** Planned, In Progress, Achieved (for past milestones)

**Visual Elements:**
- Timeline line connecting milestones
- Milestone markers (circles, icons)
- Phase color coding (light green gradient)
- Icons for different initiative types
- Connection between related initiatives

**Interactive Features:**
- Clickable milestones for expanded details
- Expandable descriptions
- Milestone filtering by area/type
- Optional: Toggle between timeline views

**Design Notes:**
- Responsive: Vertical on mobile, horizontal on desktop
- Visually compelling and easy to scan
- Sage green primary color
- Golden/amber accents for key milestones
- Clear date labels

### 5. Strategic Goals & Objectives
- **Status:** Required
- **Format:** Organized sections or cards
- **Content (1-2 per expansion area):**

#### Goal Categories:
- **Environmental Goals:** Tree plantation targets, forest restoration, biodiversity
- **Social Goals:** Communities reached, people trained, livelihoods improved
- **Educational Goals:** Schools engaged, students impacted, knowledge generated

#### Goal Format:
- **Goal Statement:** Clear, measurable
- **Target Year:** When goal will be achieved
- **Current Status:** Where we are now
- **Progress Needed:** Gap to close
- **Success Metrics:** How we'll measure
- **Roadmap:** Steps to achieve goal

**Design Notes:**
- Goals organized by category
- Progress bars (visual % to goal)
- Measurement framework visible
- Inspiring but realistic targets

## Design & Visual Approach

### Color Usage
- **Headers:** Sage green (#6B8E23)
- **Timeline line:** Sage green (#6B8E23)
- **Phase markers:** Gradient from light to dark green
- **Key milestones:** Golden/amber (#FBBF24) accents
- **Text emphasis:** Sage green
- **Card backgrounds:** Light gray or white
- **Progress bars:** Green gradient

### Layout
- **Max content width:** 1200px
- **Timeline width:** Full width with padding
- **Card grids:** 2 columns (desktop), 1 column (mobile)
- **Section padding:** 64px vertical (desktop), 32px (mobile)

### Typography
- **Section heading (H2):** Serif, 36px, sage green
- **Vision statement:** Serif, 24px, sage green
- **Goal titles (H4):** Sans-serif, 20px, sage green
- **Timeline year/phase:** Sans-serif, 18px, bold, sage green
- **Body text:** Sans-serif, 16px, dark gray
